How AGAPAY Handles an Emergency

From SOS report to case resolution, here is the complete verified emergency workflow.

Step 1

Incident is Reported by Resident

Residents submit an emergency report with location, details, and photo proof through the app.

Step 2

Admin Verifies the Emergency

The admin reviews the report, checks the evidence, and validates if the incident is a legitimate emergency before dispatching responders.

Step 3

Verified Case Appears on Responder Dashboard

Once approved, the incident becomes visible to responders with complete victim and location details.

Step 4

Responder Navigates to the Location

The system provides real-time map tracking and optimal route guidance to the incident site.

Step 5

Arrival is Confirmed via GPS

Responders confirm their arrival with timestamp and GPS verification inside the app.

Step 6

Rescue Operation is Ongoing

The case status updates while responders perform assistance and rescue operations.

Step 7

Case is Resolved and Logged

After successful response, the responder confirms resolution and the system logs the case as completed.

Built for Communities. Designed for Responders.
